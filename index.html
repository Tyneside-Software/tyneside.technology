For profit — on purpose

This is a business. We source, test, and sell computers, laptops, and related kit that still work. Not landfill. Not “as seen” junk. Honest second-hand at prices that make sense for people who need access, not status.

Paying customers keep the flywheel turning: more gear in, more gear out, more surplus for the appeals pot. The shop is static (GitHub Pages) — Buy Now opens a Tide payment link or WhatsApp.

Profit with a job

Surplus is not a founder lifestyle fund. It goes to good stories — cases where free (or nearly free) equipment is the right answer: family starting over, student locked out of schoolwork, someone job-hunting without a machine.

We run an appeals system so that path is open and fair, not a silent favour.

Donate or pass on hardware

What “still working” means

  • Boots and runs — not project boards for hobbyists only
  • Honest condition — scuffs and age are fine; lying is not
  • Wiped and ready — clean install / reset where appropriate
  • Fit for purpose — web, documents, learning, job apps
  • Local first — Howden Community Hub base; expand as stock allows

Appeals — free gear for good stories

Paid sales keep the lights on and the stock moving. Appeals are how surplus becomes dignity.

How to appeal

Message on WhatsApp (preferred) or email. Tell us, in plain language:

  • What you need (laptop, desktop, screen…)
  • What you will use it for
  • Why low-cost still is not possible right now
  • Anything that helps us prioritise (family, work, study, crisis)

How we decide

We balance stock, surplus from sales, and urgency of need. Not every appeal can be met. When we can help, we will — and we will say so clearly if we cannot yet.

Good stories are not marketing. They are the reason the profit exists.
